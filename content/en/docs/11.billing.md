## **Enterprise Plans**

Blue offers custom enterprise plans tailored to your organization's specific needs. Our enterprise solutions include:

- **Custom pricing** based on your organization size and requirements
- **Annual contracts** with flexible payment terms
- **Volume discounts** for larger teams
- **Dedicated support** and onboarding

## **How Enterprise Billing Works**

### **Annual Contracts**

Enterprise customers typically work with annual or multi-year contracts that provide:

- **Predictable budgeting** with fixed annual costs
- **Flexible payment terms** (annual, quarterly, or custom schedules)
- **Invoice-based billing** instead of credit card charges
- **Custom terms** aligned with your procurement process

### **User-Based Licensing**

Our enterprise licensing model provides:

- **Unlimited users** within your contracted tier
- **Flexible user types** with custom role definitions
- **No per-user charges** for adding team members
- **Scalability** to grow without incremental costs

### **Contract Management**

Your dedicated account manager will:

- Review usage and needs before renewal
- Provide quarterly business reviews
- Ensure you're getting maximum value from Blue
- Coordinate any contract modifications as your needs change

### **Adding Users**

With enterprise plans, adding users is simple and flexible:

- **No additional charges** for adding users within your contracted tier
- **Instant provisioning** for new team members
- **Bulk user import** capabilities for large teams
- **SSO integration** for seamless onboarding
- **Custom onboarding** support for new departments or teams

### **User Management Flexibility**

Enterprise plans provide complete flexibility in user management:

- **Add or remove users** at any time without billing impact
- **Reassign licenses** as team members change roles
- **Deactivate accounts** while preserving data and history
- **Compliance tools** for user access auditing
- **Advanced permissions** for granular access control

### **Enterprise Support**

All enterprise customers receive:

- **Dedicated account management**
- **Priority support** with guaranteed response times
- **Custom training programs** for your team
- **Regular business reviews** to ensure success
- **Direct access** to our product and engineering teams

To learn more about enterprise billing options, contact our sales team at [enterprise@blue.cc](mailto:enterprise@blue.cc) or [book a demo](/demo) to see how Blue can transform your organization's workflow.
