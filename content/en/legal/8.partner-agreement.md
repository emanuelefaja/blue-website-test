**Last Updated:** August 4th, 2025

This Partner Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Blue ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") and the party identified in the Partner Application ("Partner," "you," or "your").

## 1. Partner Program Overview

### 1.1 Program Tiers
Blue offers three partnership tiers with increasing benefits and commission rates:

- **Bronze Partner**: Base tier - 25% commission rate
- **Silver Partner**: 33% commission rate (minimum 5 referrals or $500 in commissions)
- **Gold Partner**: 42% commission rate (minimum 10 referrals or $1,000 in commissions)

### 1.2 Partnership Types
Partners may operate as:
- **Direct Resellers**: Selling Blue under the Blue brand
- **White-Label Partners**: Selling Blue under Partner's own brand
- **Referral Partners**: Referring customers to Blue

### 1.3 Geographic Scope
- System Integrators may be granted exclusive geographic territories
- Other partner types operate on a non-exclusive basis globally
- Partners may work with Blue competitors

## 2. Commission Structure

### 2.1 Commission Rates
- Commissions are calculated as a percentage of revenue received by Blue from the referred customer
- Commission rates apply to the customer's lifetime value
- Commissions include customer upgrades, downgrades, and add-ons (additional users, storage)
- Service work not provided by Blue is excluded from commissions

### 2.2 White-Label Pricing
White-label partners pay Blue a fixed price based on standard per-user fees as separately agreed.

### 2.3 Payment Terms
- Commissions paid monthly in US Dollars
- Minimum payment threshold: $200 (amounts below roll to next month)
- Payment made 30 days after Blue receives customer payment
- For direct referrals: Blue invoices customers directly
- For resellers/white-label: Partners invoice their customers directly
- Partners responsible for their own tax obligations
- No tax withholding by Blue

### 2.4 Tracking and Attribution
- 180-day cookie duration for referral tracking
- Unique partner URLs for attribution
- Double-sided incentives through trackable coupon codes

## 3. Partner Obligations

### 3.1 General Requirements
- Maintain professional representation of Blue
- Accurately describe Blue's capabilities
- Not make warranties beyond Blue's standard terms
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

### 3.2 White-Label Specific
- White-label partners have full control over their branding and marketing
- No requirement to mention "Powered by Blue"
- No approval required for positioning or marketing materials

### 3.3 Customer Support
- Partners provide first-line support to their customers
- Blue provides second-line technical support
- Gold Partners receive dedicated systems engineer support (billed separately)

### 3.4 Reporting
- Live reporting accessible at partners.blue.cc via dedicated portal
- Real-time visibility into commissions, referrals, and performance metrics
- Quarterly business reviews for Silver and Gold partners

## 4. Blue's Obligations

### 4.1 Platform Access
- Provide access to Blue platform for customer provisioning
- Partner portal for tracking commissions and performance
- API key generation capabilities for technical integration

### 4.2 Marketing Support
- Marketing materials and sales collateral
- Partner listing on Blue website (optional)
- Gold Partners eligible for joint marketing fund contributions

### 4.3 Training and Certification
- Initial partner onboarding
- Product training and certification programs
- Regular updates on new features

## 5. Intellectual Property

### 5.1 Blue IP
- Blue retains all rights to its platform and technology
- Partners receive limited license to use Blue trademarks for marketing
- License terminates upon Agreement termination

### 5.2 Partner Improvements
- Any improvements or suggestions provided by Partner become Blue's property
- Partner waives all rights to such improvements

### 5.3 White-Label Rights
- White-label partners may use their own branding
- Underlying technology remains Blue's property

## 6. Data Protection and Privacy

### 6.1 Compliance
Partners must comply with all applicable data protection laws including GDPR and CCPA as outlined in our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy) and [GDPR Compliance](/legal/gdpr) documentation.

### 6.2 Data Processing
When handling customer data, Partners must adhere to the requirements in our [Data Processing Agreement](/legal/dpa).

### 6.3 Security
Partners must maintain appropriate security measures as described in our [Security](/legal/security) documentation.

## 7. Term and Termination

### 7.1 Term
- Initial term of 12 months
- Automatically renews for successive 12-month periods

### 7.2 Termination
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice
- Immediate termination for:
  - Material breach not cured within 15 days
  - Bankruptcy or insolvency
  - Actions damaging Blue's reputation

### 7.3 Post-Termination
- Partner cannot accept new customers
- Existing customers continue with commissions paid
- Return of Blue confidential information
- Survival of confidentiality, IP, and indemnification provisions

### 7.4 Clawback
Blue may reclaim commissions if Partner solicits Blue employees or customers within 12 months post-termination.

## 8. Relationship and Conflicts

### 8.1 Independent Contractor
Partners are independent contractors, not employees, agents, or joint venturers.

### 8.2 Existing Customers
- Current Blue direct customers remain with Blue
- Blue may introduce Partners for additional service work

### 8.3 Non-Solicitation
Neither party shall solicit the other's employees or contractors during the term and for 12 months after termination.

## 9. Confidentiality

### 9.1 Confidential Information
Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other's proprietary information and use it solely for Agreement purposes.

### 9.2 Exceptions
Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that:
- Is publicly available
- Was rightfully known prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed
- Must be disclosed by law

## 10. Indemnification and Liability

### 10.1 Mutual Indemnification
Each party indemnifies the other against claims arising from:
- Breach of this Agreement
- Negligence or willful misconduct
- Violation of applicable laws

### 10.2 Partner Indemnification
Partner specifically indemnifies Blue for:
- Promises or warranties beyond Blue's standard terms
- White-label partner's use of their own branding
- Any customer disputes arising from Partner's actions

### 10.3 Limitation of Liability
- Neither party liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages
- Blue's maximum liability limited to commissions paid in prior 12 months
- Exclusions do not apply to indemnification obligations

## 11. Dispute Resolution

### 11.1 Arbitration
All disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Delaware under AAA Commercial Arbitration Rules.

### 11.2 Legal Fees
Each party bears its own legal fees and costs.

### 11.3 Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by Delaware law without regard to conflicts of law principles.

## 12. General Provisions

### 12.1 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements.

### 12.2 Amendments
Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

### 12.3 Assignment
Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other's written consent, except in connection with a merger or acquisition.

### 12.4 Notices
All notices must be in writing and sent to the addresses provided in the Partner Application.

### 12.5 Severability
If any provision is found unenforceable, the remainder continues in full effect.

### 12.6 Force Majeure
Neither party is liable for delays due to circumstances beyond their reasonable control.

## 13. Gold Partner Additional Benefits

Gold Partners receive:
- Priority technical support
- Early access to new features
- Dedicated systems engineer (billed separately)
- Eligibility for joint marketing fund
- Quarterly business reviews with Blue leadership

## 14. Acceptance

By submitting a Partner Application or beginning to promote Blue, Partner agrees to be bound by this Agreement.
