The Blue Deal Registration Portal is your gateway to securing exclusive client relationships and tracking your enterprise opportunities. Once you register a deal, that client becomes exclusively yours in our system - protecting your investment in building the relationship.

Through the portal, you can:

  • Register new enterprise opportunities
  • Protect your client relationships with exclusivity
  • Track deal progress and status

Deal registration operates on a first-come, first-served basis. Once you've successfully registered a client, they're yours exclusively - even if they approach Blue directly later.

This portal is for existing Blue partners only.
