These six values shape how we build Blue, how we work together, and how we serve our customers. They're not just words on a pagethey're the foundation of everything we do.

Simplicity

Anyone can make things complex. We strive to make things as simple as they should be, but no simpler.

Long Term Thinking

We make decisions based on the long term  even if that means hard work or difficult choices.

Clarity

We prioritize ideas that are easy to understand and convey to anyone.

Attention to Detail

The small things are the big things. We take pride in our attention to detail.

Customer Obsession

We don't have investors, so we must have an incredible focus on our customers.

Deep Work

We value collaboration, but we know that quality requires time working alone, deeply.

Values in Action

How We Build

  • Every feature starts with clarityif we can't explain it simply, we don't build it
  • We obsess over details that customers will never notice but will always feel
  • Deep work sessions produce our best innovationswe protect time for thinking

How We Serve

  • Customer feedback drives our roadmapwe build what teams actually need
  • We make decisions that benefit customers for decades, not quarters
  • Simplicity is our superpowerwe remove complexity, never add it
