This document reflects the philosophy we've developed since our founding in 2018, including extensive feedback from over 15,000 organizations across 120+ countries. As the future of work continues to evolve, our Charter will guide us in acting in the best interests of teams and organizations worldwide.

Our Mission

Blue's mission is to organize the world's work by building the best project management platform on the planet—simple, powerful, flexible, and affordable for all.

We build tools that democratize access to sophisticated work management capabilities, ensuring that every team—regardless of size, budget, or technical expertise—can benefit from better organization and collaboration.

To that end, we commit to the following principles:

Democratized Access

We commit to ensuring that powerful work organization tools remain accessible to all teams, not just well-funded enterprises. We will resist the industry trend toward increasingly complex and expensive solutions that exclude smaller organizations.

Our primary obligation is to our customers and their success. As a self-funded company, we maintain complete independence from investor pressures that might compromise our commitment to affordability, simplicity, or long-term thinking. Every decision will be evaluated through the lens of customer benefit.

At $7 per user per month, we provide 80% of enterprise functionality at 20% of the cost of our competitors, ensuring that growing teams can access world-class project management without breaking their budgets.

Sustainable Simplicity

We are committed to building solutions that are "as simple as they should be, but no simpler." We will actively resist feature bloat and complexity that serves no genuine customer need.

We recognize that in a world of unlimited venture capital funding, there is competitive pressure to over-engineer solutions. We commit to disciplined product development—every feature must clear an extremely high bar of solving real customer problems before implementation.

Our advantage is that we do not have hundreds of engineers building features for the sake of building features. We do less, but better—resulting in faster onboarding, less training overhead, and higher adoption rates.

Customer-Centric Excellence

To remain effective at democratizing work organization, Blue must deliver exceptional value while maintaining our core principles of simplicity and affordability.

We believe that sustainable business practices and customer obsession create better long-term outcomes than growth-at-all-costs mentalities. We will prioritize craftsmanship, reliability, and deep customer relationships over market share or valuation metrics.

Our self-funded model ensures we can make decisions based on customer needs and long-term thinking rather than external pressures or short-term financial goals.

Collaborative Philosophy

We will actively support the broader ecosystem of work management tools and practices. We seek to elevate industry standards around simplicity, transparency, and customer focus.

We are committed to sharing knowledge that helps teams work better together. This includes our documentation, best practices, and insights about effective team collaboration—because when teams everywhere work better, everyone benefits.

Our platform adapts to your unique processes rather than forcing you to change how you work. We believe organizations should differentiate themselves through their unique business processes, not by following generic "best practices."

Our Core Values

Simplicity

We strive to make things as simple as they should be, but no simpler.

Long Term Thinking

We make decisions based on the long term—even if that means hard work or difficult choices.

Clarity

We prioritize ideas that are easy to understand and convey to anyone.

Attention to Detail

The small things are the big things. We take pride in our attention to detail.

Customer Obsession

We don't have investors, so we must have an incredible focus on our customers.

Deep Work

We value collaboration, but we know that quality requires time working alone, deeply.

Principles That Won't Change

Ease of use

As our platform evolves, we will make it simpler to use, not more complex. We will always prioritize intuitive design and clear user experiences over feature density.

Human-centered approach

Teams will always be able to reach real humans when they need support. We will never hide behind automation or complicated support hierarchies.

Independence

Our self-funded model ensures we can make decisions based on customer needs and long-term thinking rather than external pressures or short-term financial goals.

Quality over speed

We will ship features when they meet our standards for reliability and usefulness, not to meet arbitrary deadlines or competitive pressures.

This Charter represents our commitment to building not just a successful company, but a responsible steward of how teams organize their most important work.

When organizations choose Blue, they're not just selecting a tool—they're partnering with a company dedicated to their long-term success, backed by the trust of over 15,000 organizations worldwide.
